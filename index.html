What We Do

Full-Service Repairs
for Every Vehicle

From routine maintenance to complex mechanical repairs, D's Auto Repair handles it all. Honest diagnostics, quality parts, and fair labor rates — every time, for every make and model.

Oil Changes & Fluid Service
Conventional, synthetic, and high-mileage oil changes. Includes fluid top-off and basic vehicle inspection with every visit.
Brake Repair & Service
Brake pad and rotor replacement, caliper service, and brake fluid flush. Restore safe stopping performance on any vehicle.
Electrical & Alternators
Alternator replacement, starter repair, battery testing and replacement, wiring diagnostics, and complete electrical system service.
Engine & General Repairs
Engine diagnostics, timing belts, water pumps, spark plugs, and full general maintenance. We fix what others overlook.
Preventive Maintenance
Scheduled tune-ups, belt and hose inspections, coolant flushes, and transmission fluid service. Keep your vehicle running longer.
Diagnostics & Inspections
Check engine light, OBD-II scans, and 21-point vehicle inspection. We tell you exactly what's wrong — no upsells, no guessing.
